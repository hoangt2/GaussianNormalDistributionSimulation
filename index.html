About this Simulation

This simulation demonstrates how the Gaussian (normal) distribution emerges naturally when random processes are stacked.

Balls fall from the top and hit pegs. Each time a ball hits a peg, it has a 50% chance to go left or right.

After many balls, the distribution of balls in buckets approximates the bell curve of a normal distribution.
